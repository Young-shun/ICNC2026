Oral Presentation Instruction

Each oral presentation is a maximum of 15 minutes total, including speaking time and discussion. Speakers should meet the session chair 10 minutes before the session starts and copy the slide file, PPT or PDF, to the computer.

Poster Presentation Instruction

Maximum poster size is 59.4 cm wide by 84.1 cm high, A1. Posters should be condensed and attractive, and the author should stay by the poster during the assigned session.
