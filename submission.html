Submission Guidelines

  1. The paper must be written in English and must be at least 4 pages long.
  2. The paper should have academic or practical value and must not have been published in any domestic or international academic journals or conferences.
  3. Authors may conduct self-funded plagiarism checks through CrossCheck, Turnitin, or other relevant systems. Any rejection resulting from excessive similarity rates shall be the sole responsibility of the authors.
  4. Papers that have been previously published in domestic or international academic journals or conferences, or are suspected of plagiarism, will be disqualified from publication.
