About ICNC

The International Conference on Neuromorphic Computing (ICNC 2026) is designed as a high-level academic platform for researchers, engineers, and industry experts working on brain-inspired computing systems.

The conference will focus on recent breakthroughs in neural networks, brain-inspired algorithms, cognitive computing, adaptive learning systems, artificial intelligence, and cluster intelligence.

Through keynote speeches, thematic symposiums, technical sessions, and poster presentations, ICNC aims to foster academic exchange, interdisciplinary innovation, and international collaboration.
