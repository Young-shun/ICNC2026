Call For Papers

The topics of interest for submission include, but are not limited to:

01

Neuromorphic Hardware & System Design

Novel neuromorphic chips; memristors and brain-inspired computing hardware; low-power neural network accelerator architectures.

02

Neural Algorithms, Models & Cognitive Intelligence

New algorithms for neural networks; fusion of deep learning and neuromorphic computing; spiking neural networks and adaptive computing models.

03

Bio-Inspired Computing and Theories

Neuroscience foundations, brain function simulation, neural coding and decoding mechanisms, synaptic plasticity and learning algorithms.

04

Application Cases and Interdisciplinary Integration

Autonomous driving, healthcare, robotics, Internet of Things, quantum computing, photonic computing, and materials science.

05

Swarm Intelligence and Intelligent Unmanned Systems

Intelligent cooperative control algorithms, large-scale decision-making, multi-agent reinforcement learning, active perception and understanding.

06

Neuromorphic Software and Tool Development

Neuromorphic computing software frameworks, simulation and emulation tools, programming languages, and compiler optimization.
